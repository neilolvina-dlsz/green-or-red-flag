Red Flag or Green Flag?

A simple game about everyday school choices. Use your webcam to decide whether a classroom habit is a good strategy or a trap!

How to Play

🔴

RED FLAG (LEAN LEFT)

Lean your upper body to the left if you think a habit is unhelpful or a bad idea.

🟢

GREEN FLAG (LEAN RIGHT)

Lean your upper body to the right if you think a habit is helpful or a smart strategy.

💡 Stand in the middle, read the card, and lean left or right. Hold your pose for 2 seconds to lock in your choice!
